Interactive hierarchical sunburst. Use Enter or Space to drill into a slice; Up Arrow, center circle, or Reset returns upward. Left / Right move between siblings; Down enters first child.

Using the Chart

  • Drill Down: Click or press Enter/Space on a slice.
  • Go Up: Click center ring, press Up, Esc, or use Reset.
  • Navigate: Left/Right = sibling slices; Down = first child.
  • Focus Path: Breadcrumb buttons jump directly to ancestors (desktop).
  • Tooltip: Hover or keyboard focus shows value + percent of total.
  • Labels: Auto shrink / truncate; full text exposed to assistive tech.
  • Export: Download PNG preserves current drill level.
Percent values stay relative to root for stable comparison.
